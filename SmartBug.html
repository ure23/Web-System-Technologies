Website Redesign: The Anatomy of a Great Homepage

February 6, 2020
By Amanda Singleton

In your home, chances are there are several points people could enter your house from—front doors.

The Purpose of Optimizing Your Homepage

Overall, the purpose of your website is to market your products or services

Before Your Start Designing, Remember...

Clearly define goals and strategies for what you want your homepage

Homepage Best Practices

1. State Your Value Proposition

  • What is this product or service?
  • When I'm on this website, where should I go first?

    • 2. Persona-Focused Design and Copy

    Cater to the audience you are trying to attract:

  • Define your persona's pain points
  • Offer solutions

    • 3. Navigation

    Navigation is the most essential aspect.

  • User-friendly
  • Limit the number of top-level navigation items

    • 4. Calls to Action

    Help move website visitors down the funnel in their buying process with the following CTAs:

  • Awareness CTAs: These calls to action should link to a landing page offering
  • Consideration CTAs: These CTAs generally link to pieces of content

    • CTA placement is also important on your homepage.

    5. Link to Popular Content

    Build trust and credibility with your visitors by linking to popular content on your homepage.

    6. Colors

    Pick two to four colors for your website

    7. Responsive

    A website that is designed to adapt to whatever device
